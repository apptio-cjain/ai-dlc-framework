# Functional Design

## Purpose
**Detailed business logic design per unit**

Functional Design focuses on:
- Detailed business logic and algorithms for the unit
- Domain models with entities and relationships
- Detailed business rules, validation logic, and constraints
- Technology-agnostic design (no infrastructure concerns)

## Prerequisites
- Units Generation must be complete
- Unit of work artifacts must be available
- Application Design recommended

## Steps to Execute

1. **Analyze Unit Context** - Read unit definition and assigned stories
2. **Create Functional Design Plan** - Generate plan with checkboxes
3. **Generate Context-Appropriate Questions** - Ask about business logic, domain model, business rules, data flow, integration points, error handling
4. **Store Plan** - Save as functional-design-plan.md
5. **Collect and Analyze Answers** - Wait for user, analyze for ambiguities
6. **Generate Functional Design Artifacts** - Create business-logic-model.md, business-rules.md, domain-entities.md
7. **Present Completion Message** - Show review required
8. **Wait for Explicit Approval**
9. **Record Approval and Update Progress**
